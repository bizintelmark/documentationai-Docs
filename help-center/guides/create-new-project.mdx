## Overview

Projects help organize your marketing documents.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    ````
    Go to https://dashboard.bizintelmarketing.com and sign in.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Create Project'">
    ````
    On your dashboard, press the "Create Project" button.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter project details">
    ````
    Name your project, select a template (optional), and click "Save".
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start adding documentation">
    Begin creating and organizing your content as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
