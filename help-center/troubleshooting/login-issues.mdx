---
title: Can't log in to Biz Intel Marketing
description: Resolve log in problems quickly.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to access dashboard
- "Invalid credentials" or no response after login

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
    Check your email for the reset link and follow instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email & Password">
    Make sure there are no typos and Caps Lock is off.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try refreshing or clearing cookies if access still fails.
  </Step>
</Steps>
